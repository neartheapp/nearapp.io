Frequently Asked Questions

How does Near detect nearby users? +
Near checks your GPS location approximately once per minute. When another Near user is within 0.1 miles of you, they appear on your daily nearby list. Your exact location is never shared with other users.
Will Near drain my battery? +
Near is designed to be battery efficient. It only checks your location once per minute and automatically pauses when you haven't moved. You can also set Active Hours in Settings to limit when location tracking runs.
Can other users see my exact location? +
No. Other users only know that you were within 0.1 miles of them at some point during the day. Your exact GPS coordinates are never visible to anyone on the platform.
When does my nearby list reset? +
Your nearby list resets every day at 4:00 AM in your local time zone. All location data is permanently deleted at this time. Premium users can see 3 days of history.
How do I go invisible? +
Go to your Profile tab and toggle off "Make me discoverable." You'll immediately disappear from everyone's nearby list but can still see other users. You can also toggle this quickly from the Settings tab.
How does the DM system work? +
Free users get 1 cold DM per day. If the recipient doesn't reply, you cannot DM them again. If they do reply, the conversation is unlocked with no limits. Premium users get 15 cold DMs per day with no ads required.
My account was suspended — what do I do? +
If you believe your account was suspended in error, please email support@nearapp.io with your username and a description of the situation. We review all appeals within 48 hours.
How do I cancel my subscription? +
Subscriptions are managed through the App Store (iPhone) or Google Play (Android). Go to your device's subscription settings to cancel. Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing period.
How do I delete my account? +
Go to Profile → Settings → Account → Delete Account. Your data will be permanently removed within 30 days. This action cannot be undone.
How do I report someone? +
On any user's profile page, tap the "..." menu in the top right corner and select "Report this person." Choose a reason and submit. Our team reviews all reports.
