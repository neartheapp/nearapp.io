HOW IT WORKS

Stop wondering. Start knowing.

Near runs quietly in the background, detecting other users within 0.1 miles of you throughout your day.

01
Enable location

Near checks your location once per minute. Your exact location is never shared with anyone — ever.

02
Cross paths

When another Near user comes within 0.1 miles of you, they're added to your daily nearby list.

03
Discover & connect

Browse who was near you today. Unlock their socials, send a message, or just satisfy your curiosity.

04
Resets at 4am

Every day at 4am your list resets. Fresh start. New day. New people to discover.
